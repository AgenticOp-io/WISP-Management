## 1) Long-form launch post (recommended for day one)

> 🚀 Today I’m launching **AgenticOps.io** — and the engine that makes it real:
> **Chrysalis**.
>
> For two years I’ve been quietly proving a thesis: agents can carry far more of the
> engineering work than the discourse suggests — but **only if the system can prove
> what they’ve done**. Vibes don’t belong in production. Verification does.
>
> The proving ground was **wisptools.io** — a multi-tenant LTE/ISP operations
> platform with Voice/SIP &amp; UC, HSS, CBRS, TR-069/GenieACS, ArcGIS mapping, and a
> Bandwidth Test Manager. Built solo. Shipped to production. Open-sourced under MIT.
> The discipline that fell out of it became Chrysalis.
>
> 🦋 **Chrysalis** is the verification-backed migration engine under the hood:
> oracle corpus, automated replay, chimera cutover. Not vibe-coded rewrites —
> proof-grounded translation. Currently in private alpha; an MIT open-source
> release is planned.
>
> ⚙️ **AgenticOps** is the operating model that wraps it. The contract is simple:
>
> 🤝 **Humans own outcomes** — scope, risk, business decisions.
> 🤖 **Agents accelerate exploration** — refactors, tests, runbooks, migrations.
> 🛡️ **Systems enforce correctness** — oracle replay, behavioral diff, chimera cutover.
>
> Three ways to bring it in:
>
> • **AgenticOps Pilot** — fixed scope, 4–8 weeks. One slice of your app, end-to-end.
>   You leave with proof the method works on your stack.
> • **AgenticOps Run** — retainer for ongoing modernization with the verify loop
>   running every day.
> • **AgenticOps Enablement** — workshop on your code, not slideware. Install the
>   discipline in your team’s muscle memory.
>
> If you’re a CTO or VP Eng staring down a legacy stack — PHP, Perl, classic .NET,
> a monolith you can’t safely cut up — this is the loop. Pilots are open.
>
> 👉 https://agenticop.io
> 🔧 Production proof: https://wisptools.io
> 📩 Brief on Chrysalis access: hello@agenticop.io
>
> #AgenticOps #Chrysalis #LegacyModernization #AgenticAI #AIEngineering #VerifiedMigrations #VPEngineering #CTO #BuildInPublic

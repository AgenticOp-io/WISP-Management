What is AgenticOps

A way of running serious change — not a wrapper around a model.

The way ambitious software gets shipped is being rewritten. Months of typing, ticket queues, and quarter-long handoffs are artifacts of the era when humans authored every line. Agentic operations collapses that loop — but only if the system can tell you when an agent is wrong.

AgenticOps is the operating model for that shift. Humans own outcomes: scope, risk, business decisions. Agents accelerate exploration: refactors, tests, runbooks, migrations, documentation. Systems enforce correctness: oracle replay, behavioral diffs, chimera cutover, audit trails.

Agents belong in repair, insight, documentation, test expansion, and runbook drafting — not as the sole authority on correctness. That’s what Chrysalis is for.

“Agents alone are not safe on production code. Verification is. AgenticOps wires the two together.”

— Founder, AgenticOps

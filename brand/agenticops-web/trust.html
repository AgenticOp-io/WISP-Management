Trust

What we won’t ship — by design.

Confidence with agents in production comes from guardrails you can name. These are ours, baked into Chrysalis and every AgenticOp.io engagement.

No silent wrong output

If the new code disagrees with the oracle, the merge fails. There is no “mostly works.” Behavior is recorded, replayed, and diffed.

Honest partial translation

When a slice can’t be fully translated, the gaps — “holes” — are surfaced explicitly with rationale, not hidden behind a green checkmark.

Human-in-the-loop on cutover

Chimera deploys are explicit. Traffic shifts are gated, observable, and reversible. Humans approve. Agents execute. The system records.

Audit trail for every action

Every agent decision — what it read, what it changed, what it merged — is logged, attributable, and replayable.

Security stays your team’s

We integrate with your secrets management, your CI, your review process. Self-hosted Chrysalis available on Run engagements.

Open core, not lock-in

Chrysalis source is on GitHub (early). The corpus, the verify gate, the cutover orchestration — yours to inspect and fork; confirm license in the repo’s LICENSE file.

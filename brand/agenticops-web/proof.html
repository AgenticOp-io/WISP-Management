Chrysalis — the verification-backed migration engine

Chrysalis is the proof layer underneath every AgenticOps engagement. It records what your legacy actually does, replays that behavior against any candidate implementation, surfaces the gaps honestly, and orchestrates a chimera cutover so old and new run side-by-side until correctness is earned. Not vibe-coded rewrites — proof-grounded translation.

Capabilities

  • Oracle corpus capture
  • Deterministic replay harness
  • Behavioral diff & coverage
  • Honest partial translation (“holes”)
  • Chimera cutover orchestration

Where it fits

  • Legacy PHP / Perl / classic .NET
  • Monolith → service decomposition
  • Language & framework migrations
  • Risk-bounded refactors at scale

Status

wisptools.io — production proof of the AgenticOps method

Before AgenticOps was a brand, it was a discipline — and wisptools.io was the proving ground. A multi-tenant operations platform for WISP / ISP networks: LTE/5G planning, Voice / SIP & UC, HSS subscribers, CBRS, TR-069/GenieACS, ArcGIS mapping, and a Bandwidth Test Manager. Architected and shipped end-to-end with agents, in production, MIT-licensed.

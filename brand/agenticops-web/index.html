What is AgenticOps

One system at a time — verified transformation that compounds.

Big-bang rewrites fail because nobody can hold the whole graph in their head. AgenticOps transforms one system at a time: a bounded slice of your legacy estate, an oracle that records truth, a candidate that must replay it, a chimera cutover that earns traffic — then we repeat on the next bounded system.

Chrysalis is the first flagship — verification-backed migration on legacy PHP (capture → ingest → emit → verify → chimera). Source: github.com/4GEngineer/chrysalis (its own repo, not buried inside WISP-Management). It’s early; pilots stay private alpha and scoped one system at a time. wisptools.io remains the separate, production-hardened proof of how we ship with agents.

The north star stays systemic fragility — coupling, contagion, governance shocks — where silent wrong output becomes a crisis. AgenticOps states intent; Chrysalis earns credibility one legacy system at a time in software first.

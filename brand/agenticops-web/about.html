About

From hardware to autonomy — with a verification habit.

AgenticOps is led by an engineer with decades in the trenches — from the hardware-heavy world of LTE radios to multi-region cloud platforms and production telecom. The thesis was simple: agents can carry far more of the work, but only if the system can prove what they’ve done.

wisptools.io proved the operating model. Chrysalis is the engine that came out of it — and the reason AgenticOps is willing to put agents anywhere near production code. Humans own outcomes. Agents accelerate exploration. Systems enforce correctness.
